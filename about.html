My Story

Growing up, my first passion was art. I loved to collect sketchbooks and fill them up with my cartoon drawings. Through grade school I was always enrolled in an art class, and that followed me to college as well. I didn't major in Art, however after I graduated college and after spending some time as an educator, I reconnected with art by way of UI design. I took a bootcamp course through careerfoundry and learned all about UI design. As a hobby I teach myself how to create digital art using Procreate. Aside from Art and design, I love being in and around the water and I enjoy spending time in coffee shops.
